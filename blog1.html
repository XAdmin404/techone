How did I learn programming? What was the process I went through and what did I learn?

24 march 2021


The first interactions with technology.



My passion for computers dates back to my childhood. Ever since I was little, I used to button the computer, explore everything in it, walk through folders and create folders, etc. At the age of 8 I had my first tablet, which obviously I started to button. At first I played games, but over time I learned how things are and I started walking through it, having a burning desire to uninstall its system applications, eventually succeeding. The tablet and computer were my best friends for years to come. Two years later, I also had my first phone, where I used to play minecraft, I played with hacks, I always cheated. In I had become advanced in terms of operating systems. I was obsessed with minecraft. In 2016, having a weak computer, I wanted to play minecraft, so I tried to make my cardboard computer work. I had started to install virtual machines, at that time I had learned to use CMD etc.


Programming itself


In 2019, I found an ad on youtube, which introduced you to a free programming course. I started it, but I didn't really understand anything. It was a c ++ course, and just because I didn't understand anything, I dropped it. In 2020, at the beginning of the year, I resumed that course, where I laid the foundations of c ++. I learned about variables, operators, if and else, while for etc. After three months, I was able to write simple programs in c ++, and in the summer of 2020 I continued with algorithms. Also in the summer, I made my first website, but I did it a little because I did it after a tutorial on youtube, so it wasn't really done by me. I continued with algorithms and c ++ until the winter, when I started the curriculum after freecodecamp of responsive web design, which I recommend to everyone, where I continued until March this year when I took that certification, and I learned to create anything myself Web page. Now continue with JavaScript because I want to advance and create complex web applications.
